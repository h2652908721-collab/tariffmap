The Problem

Wrong HS Code = You Pay the Bill

⚠️

Misclassification triggers fines & delays

Customs authorities audit post-clearance. A wrong HS code doesn't just mean the wrong duty rate — it means penalties, hold-and-examine delays, and potential seizure. One reclassification can trigger review of all prior shipments.

📊

Tariff rates change — and they change fast

Section 301 tariffs, anti-dumping measures, TRQ thresholds, FTA phase-outs. What was 0% last year might be 25% today. Official schedules are thousands of pages long and updated without notice.

💸

You end up overpaying or underpaying — both are problems

Overpay and you're leaving margin on the table. Underpay and you face back-duty demands with interest. Either way, the cost of getting it wrong is always higher than the cost of getting it right the first time.
